About the Program

As a part of WTM Winter Mentorship Program, student participants will learn under a mentor on one of the Web, Dev or Android tracks, gaining exposure to real-world programming/development and techniques. Students have the opportunity to gain valuable experience while working in areas related to their interests.

There is also a provision to propose a new project (check the template) under Android and Web Tracks. Here students can propose an idea or a project they would like to have mentorship assistance.
Our mentors are some of the best from the college and have worked on several projects in their domains extensively.

How the mentorship works?

Register for the Mentorship Program even if you want to propose your project (it is first come first serve).
It is a remote mentorship program and your mentors will guide you through the learning process(which includes real life projects) for a period of 2-6 weeks; depending on the track and speed.
Once the internship period ends, the mentors will announce top 3 performers and they will be awarded with certificates and prizes. Everyone else who completes will be given a completion certificate.

Guidelines

  • Students must be enrolled at Manipal Institute of Technology to be eligible
  • Open to students of all years and all branches
  • If you don't have one, please create a GitHub account
