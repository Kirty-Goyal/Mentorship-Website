by Rakshit Thakkar

Duration: 5-6 weeks

Overview

Android is a mobile operating systems, developed and maintained by Google. Android works on a multitude of devices like mobile, tablets, PCs, cars, smart watches, smart TVs. Itâ€™s open-source nature allows billions of people worldwide to customize their phones any way they want to.

Why Android

Developing applications for Android is very easy and Google itself provides a ton of guidelines and materials that developers can use. Android Studio is the primary and preferred IDE used for developing Android apps for all devices. Android development is also the second most popular field of work for freelancers as it is accessible to anyone.
In this mentorship drive, we will introduce interested students to Android application development, and how it can impact lives all around the world.
